About Flick Finder

Flick Finder is a modern movie recommendation app. Select your favorite genres, desired average rating, and number of votes to get personalized movie suggestions powered by AI.

  • Choose one or more genres
  • Set your preferred average rating (0-10)
  • Set the minimum number of votes (0-1000)
  • Get a list of recommended movies!

Created for movie lovers.
